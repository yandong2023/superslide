Frequently Asked Questions

What is Superslide (Klotski)?

Superslide, also known as Klotski or 华容道, is a classic sliding block puzzle game that originated in ancient China. The goal is to move blocks around to help the largest block reach the exit.

How do I play?

Simply drag the blocks with your mouse or finger to move them. Blocks can only move horizontally and vertically, and they cannot overlap. Try to get the largest block to the exit in as few moves as possible!

Is there a minimum number of moves?

Yes! Each puzzle has a theoretical minimum number of moves needed to solve it. Part of the challenge is finding the most efficient solution.

Can I play on mobile?

Absolutely! Superslide is fully optimized for both desktop and mobile devices. You can play using touch controls on your smartphone or tablet.
